Sakura Blossom

The cherry blossom (桜, sakura) is Japan's unofficial national flower. It has been celebrated for many centuries and a very prominent position in Japanese culture.
The seasonal spectacle is celebrated with hanami (cherry blossom viewing) parties under the trees.
